"You have the right to work only, but never to its fruits.
Let not the fruits of action be your motive, nor let your attachment be to inaction."

This teaching from Chapter 2, Verse 47 transformed my perspective on life. I learned to focus on my actions without attachment to results, finding peace in doing my duty while leaving outcomes to the divine. It changed how I approach challenges and successes alike.
